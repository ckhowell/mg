Quality

Our Mirrors are A Grade Quality silver float glass. Thickness from either 4mm and 6mm.

All quality checked, glass is free from waves and imperfections. Larger mirrors will be 6mm, thicker glass which helps make them more rigid and reduces the possibility of breaking.

We use an edge sealant, a specially formulated clear coating to reduce silver deterioration.
