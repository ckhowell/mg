Frameless Glass Showerscreens are custom designed for elegance and simplicity and add a touch of luxury to your bathroom. Our screens are Australian made and custom installed to your exact specifications.

Barely There

Frameless glass shower screens look modern, stylish and streamlined. Moreover, a glass shower screen will transform your bathroom fully by providing a spacious look and feel and facilitate other bathroom features to stand out.

Sleek Quality Hardware

Glass-to-Glass Shower Hinge is the perfect piece for the door of an enclosed shower cavity. Suitable for glass 8-12mm thick, this fitting comes with all the required hardware and will add a touch of style to your space.

Custom made and installed

We custom-build our shower screens and wardrobes to fit perfectly, accounting for the angles of the floor and walls. For shower screens, this is necessary to prevent leaks and unsightly excess silicone work.

Make Cleaning a Breeze

Enduroshield's glass corrosion coating and protection stops corrosion by repelling water, dirt, oil, soaps and shampoo residues.
